This art portfolio is a constant snapshot of my artistic journey. For the longest time, I was a firm believer that art should be view in person to truly appreciate the artist’s style and creative vision.

Thus, I was very reluctant to document my works digitally, especially since I prefer drawing on paper over a digital screen. However, I want my artwork to reach the greatest audiences possible, and to (as my high school art teacher once said) “stand the test of time”.

So this porfolio was started and I will alway be tweaking it like I do with all my artworks.
