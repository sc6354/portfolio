FACES

My earliest complete body of pieces

circa. 2016-2018

Over the course of two+ years, this collection of portraits were all drawn on various types of paper and with H, B2, B6, B7, and B8 drawing pencils, and with black & gray color pencils.

During this period, I became interested in the human face and its portions. Although it is true that no two faces have exactly the same features, many faces do share a similar portion and and size. From this collection, I wanted to used these portions to realistically drawing these faces.

Unfortunately, this is not all the portraits I have drawn over the two+ years, but only the ones I could savage from my archives.
